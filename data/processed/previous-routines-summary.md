## Program Structure

Three mesocycles (M1, M2, M3), each with 4 weekly progressions (S1-S4).

| Mesocycle | Days/week | Main lifts | RPE progression |
|-----------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| M1 | 3 | Floor Press, Sumo DL, Incline Bench | 7 → 8 → 9 → 10 |
| M2 | 3 | Eccentric Bench, Box Squat, Eccentric Shoulder Press | 7 → 8(?) → 9 → max |
| M3 | 4 | Bench ISO 2", Deficit Back Lunge, Shoulder Press Max Effort, Barbell RDL | 7 → 8 → 9 → 10 |

## Session Template (consistent across all mesocycles)

1. **Core** — 2-4 rounds of 2-3 exercises
2. **Fuerza (strength)** — 1 main compound, ramped sets to working weight
3. **Superset partner** — lighter compound or isolation, 3-4 sets
4. **Estructura (structure)** — 2 compound/accessory pairings, 3-4 rounds each, separated by 2-4 min rest
5. **WOD (finisher)** — CrossFit-style conditioning: for-time, EMOM, or intervals
6. **Bonus pump** (some days) — 3 rounds of isolation work (arms typically)

## Progression Model

- Within each mesocycle: RPE climbs from 7 → 10 over 4 weeks
- Volume also increases slightly (rounds go from 3 to 4 in some blocks)
- Across mesocycles: exercise selection rotates, main lifts change
- No programmed deload — S4 is a max-effort week every time
