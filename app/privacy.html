Privacy Policy

Training System — Personal fitness tracking app

Last updated: 2026-04-07

Data Collection

This app collects only the data you voluntarily enter (workouts, runs, nutrition, body weight) and, if connected, physiological data from your WHOOP account (recovery, sleep, HRV, strain).

Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device (IndexedDB) and optionally synced to a private Supabase database. Your data is never shared with third parties.

WHOOP Integration

If you connect your WHOOP account, the app reads recovery, sleep, and strain data via the WHOOP API. This data is stored only in your personal database and is never shared.

Third Parties

This app does not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third party.

Contact

This is a personal-use application. For questions, contact the app owner directly.
